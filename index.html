• These are extracted and derived from the Mii Central Miis from the 3DS Miitopia, these are to my knowledge complete.
  • The code that made these images and Mii files are the result of a lot of manual work, however the website displaying them is temporary as a much better more encompassing website is being written. In the meantime, this webpage to display the data was generated using LLMs. This is in no way its final form and the final website will not be, this website is a placeholder so I can open source the data now while the main website is finished.
  • A much better and more encompassing website is being written by human hand in the background, this URL will become a redirect to that when it is fully released.
  • These are provided as is, exactly as extracted and derived, with no filtering being done. As such, examples of topics you may or may not disagree with being present, examples of topics I myself may or may not disagree with, are shown here exactly as extracted and derived from the real list you may come across in gameplay. This can include politics, cuss words, and other sensitive topics.
  • Duplicates or very nearly duplicates were present when extracting and derived, and have not been removed for accuracy.
  • The so-called "ID"s here are not the Mii ID but rather a bit offset for debugging purposes and can be completely ignored.
  • You can either scan the QR with your 3DS, or you can download the raw FFCD (same as CFCD in all ways that actually matter). When this is moved to the final main website, compatibility will be much broader.
